Bridge

This is a bridge by the West Lake with a resting building. The shooting time was about 6 PM. I accidentally photographed a couple standing on a bridge. Some golden sunsets could be seen over the hills in the distance. It was a peaceful moment

Small Pavilion

This is a small pavilion by the West Lake. This photo was taken around 2 PM. Near is the wilted lotus in winter, far is the tourists in boats to the island in the middle of the lake.

Ancestral Hall 1

This is an ancestral hall in Hangzhou, formerly a royal palace in the 'Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms' period, about 1000 years ago. This was taken outside the wall, while we were touring out on our way to the West Lake.

Ancestral Hall 2

This photo shows the inside of the ancestral hall. As we went there around the New Year's Day, so there are very few tourists.

Mid-lake Island

This is the island in the middle of West Lake. The related article "appreciating snow on the island in the middle of the lake" has been handed down from the Ming Dynasty. Hangzhou is a south city, snow is very rare. Though it was winter when we went, but it is sunny that day.

Lotus

This is the lotus by the West Lake. It blooms very well in summer. Throughout history, many writers and poets have paid tribute here. Especially in the Song Dynasty, Hangzhou, also known as Lin 'an, was the capital of the South Song Dynasty.

Cliff Carving

This is a cliff carving taken in Lingyin Temple, Hangzhou. It depicts the caravans that trafficked in goods. Stone carved around the South Song Dynasty. The South Song was a historical dynasty that focused on commerce and culture, so trade flourished.

Study Room

This is a study room. It is arranged in the Ming Dynasty style. Furniture is a typical Ming style, simple and generous. Decoration is Taihu stone, a kind of stone produced in Taihu Lake, with a strange shape and much loved by scholars in acient China.

Stone Inscription

This is a stone inscription in a cave, about the Ming or Qing Dynasties. It said that 'a contented mind is a perpetual feast'. I liked this phrase so much that I took a photo of it.

Lingyin Temple

This is the scene in Lingyin Temple on New Year's Day. Many people, both local and non-local, come to this temple specifically to pray. When I came here on the last New Year's Day before covid-19, it was very crowded and the atmosphere was very lively.

Tree and Wall

This is a random shot of a tree in Hangzhou, with a red wall in the background. This kind of subject matter is quite common in the Forbidden City in Beijing.
